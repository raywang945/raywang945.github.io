about us

Compumower Industries Inc. (Jio-wang) began as a humble trade business at Zhongxiao East Road, Section 5, Songshan District in Taipei in June 1991 by the founder Mr. Herman Wu. With the help of Manager Lu and one diligent employee, the company grew from a modest agent for outdoor machinery components and parts, such as those for mowers, chainsaws, trimmers, etc. to an internationally recognized enterprise group with globally deployed operations. The company was later moved to its current location at No 43, Wanmei Street, Section 1, Taipei City as a response to increased demands and a way to demonstrate our continued commitment to excellence.

Being an experienced manufacturer and exporter of quality machine parts, we provide a wide range of products to allow our clients the most diversified choices that meet their varying needs. To ensure product quality, we own a number of strictly supervised factories, such as plants for plastic injection and blowing (Bestop Manufacturing Co., Ltd.), rubber extrusion molding (Bobson Manufacturing Co., Ltd.), plastic and rubber mold manufacturing (Bobson Manufacturing Co., Ltd.), filter manufactory (Maxipower Filter Manufacturing Co., Ltd.), and metal sheet pressing and stamping (Chang-hong-xin Manufacturing Co., Ltd.). We also have an annual procurement plan for production and testing equipment to ensure our production capacity and pursuit of excellence are abreast with and even ahead of our expanding businesses. Strongly export-oriented, we have a solid client network across several continents, including Europe and America. We not only provide durable aftermarket parts but also offer quality-assured original components for use with OEM machines.

Over the years, our commitment to high quality products remains our top priority. We impose a strict quality control process where only qualified products are allowed to be released for shipment and free replacement is 100% guaranteed if any defect is found. Having also passed many scrupulous quality examinations by our clients in North America and Europe, our products have establishedbuilt itself a reputationname in the market and earned trust from many of our clients who speak highly of us and recognize our dedication to what we do. Our long-standing brand "Jio-wang" has been well received in the market and this has encouraged us to continue on our quest for excellence by introducing an international quality management system that serves to uphold our promise to deliver quality products and reliable services.

"Jio-wang" in Chinese translates to "lasting prosperity", which is also what we wish to bring our clients. It is our earnest hope that by partnering with us, both our clients and us can create a win-win business that not only prospers but also lasts.

Products & Distribution

1. We are an export-oriented enterprise with 60% of our clients based in North America, 30% in Europe, and 10% in other parts of the world.

2. Our main products are original and replacement parts for lawnmowers, chainsaws, snow throwers, construction hardware and machinery, agricultural machinery, ATVs, and all other related indoor and outdoor engine parts.
